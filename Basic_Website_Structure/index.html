    We can use Emmet abbreviations like:

  1. h*5 = 5 heading tags,

  2. p*5 = 5 paragraph tags,

  3. ul>li*5 = 5 list items inside a UL.

  4. ol>li*5 = 5 list items inside a OL.

  5. div*5 = 5 div tags.

  6. div>h1 = 1 heading inside a div.

  7. div>h1+p = 1 heading and 1 paragraph inside a div.

  8. div>h1+p*5 = 1 heading and 5 paragraphs inside a div.

  9. div>h1+p*5+ul>li*5 = 1 heading, 5 paragraphs, and 5 list items inside a div.

  10. div>h1+p*5+ul>li*5+ol>li*5 = 1 heading, 5 paragraphs, 5 list items inside a UL, and 5 list items inside a OL.

  11. div>h1+p*5+ul>li*5+ol>li*5+img = 1 heading, 5 paragraphs, 5 list items inside a UL, 5 list items inside a OL, and an image.
